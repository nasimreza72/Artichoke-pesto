Artichoke pesto


Preparetion time: 20 minutes
Total cooking time: nil
Makes 1¹/² cups


  • 2 tablespoons hazelnut, roasted
  • 400 g can artichoke hearts,drained
  • 1 clove garlic, chopped
  • ¹/⁴ cup(25 g) finely grated Parmesan
  • ¹/⁴ cup(60 ml) extra virgn olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh flat-leaf
    parsley

  1. Put the hazelnuts into a food processor
    and process until finely chopped. Add
    the artichokes, garlic and parmesan.
    Process until smooth. With the motor
    running, add the oil in a thin stream.
  2. Stir in the parsley and spoon into a
    serving dish. Dipper Ideas: Herb and
    garlic pitta chips; crisp lavash bread.
